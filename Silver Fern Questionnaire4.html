The information you enter into this application form will be used to determine your eligibility for the Silver Fern Job Search visa. There are a limited number of places available in the Silver Fern quota, your place is only secured once you submit and if required pay for your application.

After you have completed the online stage of the application you will be required to submit evidence you meet the Silver Fern Job Search visa requirements.

Character Requirements

 
 

This section will help us determine whether you meet our character requirements to be approved for a visa.

You will be required to provide police certificates from your country of citizenship and every country where you have lived in for more than 5 years since you attained the age of 17.

 
 
 
Have you ever been convicted of any offence for which you have been sentenced to a term of imprisonment for a term of 5 years or more, or for an indeterminate period capable of running for 5 years or more? * You must answer this question.
Have you, in the 10 years preceding this application, ever been convicted of any offence for which you have been sentenced to imprisonment for a term of 12 months or more, or for an indeterminate period capable of running for 12 months or more? * You must answer this question.
Are you permanently or currently prohibited from entering New Zealand? * You must answer this question.
Have you ever been deported from New Zealand or any other country? * You must answer this question.
Have you, for any offence(s) against the law in any country, been :
  • Charged
 * You must answer this question.
  • Convicted or found guilty
 * You must answer this question.
  • Wanted or under investigation
 * You must answer this question.
  • Excluded from or refused entry to any country
 * You must answer this question.
  • Removed from or asked to leave?
 * You must answer this question.
If yes, please provide a full explanation of the details and surrounding circumstances. Note that if you do not meet our character requirements, you will need to be granted a character waiver for your application to be approved.
