The information you enter into this application form will be used to determine your eligibility for the Silver Fern Job Search visa. There are a limited number of places available in the Silver Fern quota, your place is only secured once you submit and if required pay for your application.

After you have completed the online stage of the application you will be required to submit evidence you meet the Silver Fern Job Search visa requirements.

Silver Fern Requirements

 
 

You will be required to provide evidence you meet these criteria to Immigration New Zealand after you submit your online application. If you are unable to provide this evidence, the application may be declined.

 
 

Qualifications
How do you meet the qualification requirements for the Silver Fern Job Search visa? * You must answer this question.
Note: The New Zealand Register of Quality Assured Qualifications determines the level of qualifications. If you hold a qualification on the List of Qualifications Exempt from Assessment (LQEA) you can use this to check the level of your qualification. However if your qualification does not appear on the LQEA, you will be required to provide an International Qualification Assessment from the New Zealand Qualifications Authority to confirm the level of the qualification.
If you hold a qualification that is relevant to an occupation which requires occupational registration in New Zealand, do you hold, or can you show you are able to obtain, registration from the relevant registration authority? * You must answer this question.

English Language:
How do you meet the English language requirement for the Silver Fern Job Search visa? * You must answer this question.

Funds:
Do you have sufficient funds available for your stay in New Zealand? * You must answer this question.
