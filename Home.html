The application process

The Silver Fern Job Search visa application has two stages. In this first online stage, you will confirm that you meet the requirements for a Silver Fern Job Search visa. If there is a place available when you apply, you must pay your application fee by credit card (unless you are from a country with a work visa fee-waiver arrangement). When we have confirmed your payment, we will take a place off the quota for you

After you have completed the online stage of the application we will send you an email asking you to send us evidence that you meet the requirements. You will have three months to provide the evidence. If we don’t hear from you, your application may be declined and the fee will not be refunded.

The Silver Fern Job Search quota

There is an annual quota of places for Silver Fern Job Search visas.

Creating an online Silver Fern Job Search visa application

Before you create an online Silver Fern Job Search visa application, check that you meet the requirements,

Also note that your place will only be secured once the payment stage of the online application is completed. You will not be able to pay for and submit an application if there are no places remaining in the quota.

Once all the places in the quota have been filled we will delete unsubmitted applications. If you wish to apply again next year you will need to create a new online Silver Fern Job Search visa application.
